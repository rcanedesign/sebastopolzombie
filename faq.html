FAQ

Race Information


How old do I need to be to participate?

Since zombies are supposed to be scary we encourage you to use your best judgement when thinking of bringing your children. Under 14 years of age run for free.


What will I need with me on race day?

You will need official ID (Driver's license, Photo ID, Passport) A signed waiver.Participants under the age of 18 must bring a printed waiver signed by a parent or legal guardian to the event or we cannot let you in. Cash for food and beverages


What time should I arrive?

Packet pick up will begin at 5:00 pm. Free parking is available at the Sebastopol Veterans memorial building, in the Round Table parking area and on the streets. Please be respectful of peoples driveways and obey all parking signs.


How many zombies are there in each wave time?

Wave time? Hey let's all run this thing together!


Is the race timed?

No official timing of the race, this is for fun and for charity, so come run, walk or crawl your way to the finish line with us and we will be donating all proceeds to local children in need through the Active 20-30 Club of Sebastopol and Kaia F.I.T. Santa Rosa and Sebastopol.


Can I bring my children?

Children are welcome but keep in mind that zombies may be frightening to small children, all kids must be supervised by an adult.


Do I have to dress up?

No you do not have to dress up. But that is most of the fun!
