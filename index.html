Kaia F.I.T.

Kaia F.I.T. is a women's fitness program which incorporates eating clean and the seven everyday movements of push, pull, squat, lunge, gait, bend and twist to transform bodies into lean, strong, flexible, healthy machines. The coaches at Kaia F.I.T. are well trained in modifications for injuries or limited range of motion from years of immobility. Whether you are coming in because your Dr told you to, or a serious athlete, the coaches at Kaia will make you feel accomplished and challenged at the same time and helping you reach your full potential.


Active 20-30 Sebastopol

Active 20-30 Sebastopol is a re-charter of a club that started in Sebastopol in 1930. In an effort to expand community knowledge and participating in Active 20-30 we have been, and will continue, working with local businesses to increase membership and raise money for the children in need in West County.
