Event Details

Who:
Kaia F.I.T. Santa Rosa and Sebastopol


What:
5K Fun Run, raffle, and Health Fair!


Where:
The Barlow 6770 McKinley Sebastopol, Ca 95472


When:
Saturday October 4th Registration and packet pick up starts at 5:00pm 5K starts at about 7:00pm.


Why:
The women of Kaia FIT believe that health begins with family. Our family here in Sebastopol is our community. As a community we need to watch out for one another. Kaia FIT has teamed up with the OPEN OUR HOSPITAL Palm Drive Health Care Foundation to raise money for our hospital to be reopened! By purchasing a raffle ticket or a walking/running spot in the Zombie Run you will be gaining strength and health and helping support the community in many ways. Please do yourself and your community a favor by grabbing a family member or neighbor and joining in the fun!





Kaia F.I.T.

Kaia F.I.T. is a women's fitness program which incorporates eating clean and the seven everyday movements of push, pull, squat, lunge, gait, bend and twist to transform bodies into lean, strong, flexible, healthy machines. The coaches at Kaia F.I.T. are well trained in modifications for injuries or limited range of motion from years of immobility. Whether you are coming in because your Dr told you to, or a serious athlete, the coaches at Kaia will make you feel accomplished and challenged at the same time and helping you reach your full potential.
