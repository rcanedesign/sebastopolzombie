Rules and Safety

No smoking.
Be respectful of the people and places around you.
No weapons or mock weapons will be permitted on the premises.
No drugs or outside alcohol will be permitted on the premises.
No alcoholic beverages are to be consumed by people under the age of 21.
Participants and spectators must abide by all instructions given from the race directors, race staff, volunteers, and medical personnel.
Do not drink and drive.
All rules of the road still apply.


Zombie Stomp

Zombie Stomp will be the welcome back party for all the participants and observers. There will be a live DJ and local food vendors.
